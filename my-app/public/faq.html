FAQ - Smart Complaint Portal

1. How do I submit a complaint?

You can submit a complaint by clicking on the "Submit Complaint" button on the portal's homepage. You will need to log in using your credentials and fill in the required details to categorize and submit your complaint.

2. How can I track the status of my complaint?

Once your complaint is submitted, you can track its status by logging into the portal and navigating to the "Track Complaint Status" page. Here, you will see updates on the resolution process.

3. Who can submit a complaint?

Students, faculty, and staff members of CIT Kokrajhar can submit complaints related to campus facilities, services, and academic issues. Each user must authenticate using their institute-provided credentials.

4. What types of complaints can I submit?

You can submit complaints related to a variety of issues such as maintenance, IT support, academic concerns, student services, and other campus-related problems.

5. How will I be notified about the status of my complaint?

Notifications will be sent to your registered email address as the status of your complaint changes. You can also log in to the portal to see real-time updates.

6. How long does it take for complaints to be resolved?

Resolution times vary depending on the nature of the complaint. You will receive notifications at each stage of the resolution process, and the portal provides expected timelines for different types of complaints.

7. What should I do if I face issues using the portal?

If you encounter any technical difficulties while using the portal, please contact our IT Support team through the IT Support page for assistance.
