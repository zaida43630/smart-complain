Privacy Policy

Welcome to the Smart Complaint Portal of the Central Institute of Technology Kokrajhar (CITK). This Privacy Policy describes how we handle and protect your personal data when you use our platform. Please read this policy carefully.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information when you use the Smart Complaint Portal:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, institutional ID, and contact details provided during registration or complaint submission.
  • Complaint Information: The details of your complaint, including description, category, and status updates.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you use the portal, including login times, pages visited, and interaction data.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your personal data is used for the following purposes:

  • To process and manage complaints submitted through the portal.
  • To notify you of updates regarding the status of your complaint.
  • To analyze complaint trends and improve the services offered by CITK.
  • To ensure the security of the platform and prevent unauthorized access.

3. Data Sharing

We do not share your personal data with third parties except in the following circumstances:

  • With authorized CITK staff and administrators who need access to resolve your complaint.
  • If required by law, to comply with legal obligations or protect the rights and safety of the institution.

4. Data Security

We take the security of your data seriously. All personal information is stored securely, and we use encryption and other security measures to protect it from unauthorized access.

5. Data Retention

Your data is retained for as long as necessary to process your complaint and to meet any legal or institutional requirements.

6. User Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access to the data we hold about you.
  • Request corrections to any inaccurate or incomplete information.
  • Request the deletion of your data, where applicable.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. You will be notified of any significant changes via email or through notifications on the portal.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@citk.edu.
