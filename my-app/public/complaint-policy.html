Complaint Policy - Smart Complaint Portal

Introduction

The Smart Complaint Portal at the Central Institute of Technology Kokrajhar (CITK) is designed to provide an efficient and transparent method for students, faculty, and staff to submit and resolve complaints. This policy outlines the procedures and guidelines for complaint submission, handling, and resolution.

Who Can Submit a Complaint?

The complaint portal is available to all students, faculty members, and staff of CITK. You must authenticate with your institutional credentials before submitting a complaint.

Types of Complaints

Complaints can be related to the following areas:

  • Campus facilities (e.g., maintenance, infrastructure)
  • Student services (e.g., housing, transport, health services)
  • Academic concerns (e.g., course content, exam schedules)
  • IT and technical issues
  • Other campus-related concerns

Submission Guidelines

When submitting a complaint, please ensure the following:

  • Provide a detailed description of the issue.
  • Select the appropriate category for your complaint.
  • Include any supporting documentation or evidence if applicable.
  • Use respectful language and provide constructive feedback.

Complaint Handling Procedure

After submitting a complaint, the following process will be followed:

  • The complaint is logged into the system and assigned a unique complaint ID.
  • The appropriate department or administrator is notified of the complaint.
  • The complaint will be reviewed and an initial response will be provided within 3-5 working days.
  • If further action is required, the department will provide regular updates on the progress of the complaint resolution.
  • Once resolved, a final notification will be sent to the user, and the complaint will be closed.

Tracking Your Complaint

You can track the status of your complaint at any time by logging into the portal and navigating to the "Track Complaint Status" section. You will also receive email notifications about updates on your complaint.

Response Times

We aim to respond to complaints within 3-5 working days. Resolution times will vary depending on the nature of the complaint and the complexity of the issue.

Escalation Process

If you feel that your complaint has not been addressed adequately, you can escalate it to a higher authority within the portal. Details on how to escalate complaints are available within the "Track Complaint Status" section.

Privacy and Confidentiality

All complaints are handled with confidentiality. Personal information provided during the complaint submission process is protected under the CITK’s privacy policies. Only authorized personnel will have access to complaint details.

Amendments to the Policy

This complaint policy may be updated periodically to reflect changes in procedure. Users will be notified of significant changes via the portal.
